# Cross PR Concept

Cross PR is a Telegram mutual promotion product concept for channels, bots, creators, and communities.

The main idea is to make partner promotion easier to organize by helping owners describe their audience, find relevant matches, and structure mutual PR campaigns.

## Core Idea

Telegram channel and bot owners often need relevant partners, not random placements. Cross PR is positioned around audience-fit, mutual value, and repeatable promotion workflows.

## Public Capabilities

- describe channel or bot promotion goals
- think in terms of partner relevance
- organize mutual PR opportunities
- support promotion bundle concepts
- make outreach and campaign coordination more structured

## Why Mutual PR

Mutual PR can help Telegram projects grow through audience overlap and trust. A relevant recommendation inside another Telegram project can be more natural than a generic ad placement.

## Implementation Boundary

This document intentionally avoids matching logic, private scoring methods, prompts, partner databases, analytics workflows, and operational playbooks.
